A bright riverside café terrace curves above the water, ringed by glass railings and planters. Under large white umbrellas, small groups sit at wooden tables talking, eating, or checking phones, while a barista or waiter moves among them. A few people linger alone at the edges, and torches line the rail, unlit in the daylight. In the background, cyclists and pedestrians glide along the elevated walkway, adding gentle motion to the relaxed urban scene.
